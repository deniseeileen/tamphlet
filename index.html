About the guide and why it's different

Tamphlet is a simple guide that teaches girls how to use tampons for the first time. It displays the most needed information quickly and clearly so that each girl can have a more comfortable and confident experience.

Unlike traditional tampon instructions, tamphlet features readable text and modern illustrations. It is primarily a visual learning guide, designed with the understanding that this is a completely new experience and aims to make young girls as familiar with their bodies as possible.

Tamphlet was inspired by the common first period experiences of grown women with the hope that by sharing what we wished we had known then, future girls will be able to experience their first periods without embarrassment or discomfort.

Our Vision

  • Access

    We recognize that girls come from different backgrounds and family dynamics. Our goal is to improve access for all girls in a comfortable environment.

  • Education

    Our goal is to prevent girls from discomfort simply because they didn't know something. As we learn more about what young girls need, we will update the guide to be as effective as possible.

  • Community

    The tamphlet forum is a sharing community for girls to find the answers they need and encourages conversations without judgement.
